What's going on at Grandma's place


A little film I put together with footage filmed in 2018, from my time in the Electronic perception garage mentorship with VIVO Media Arts. I tried to touch on some of the experiences of being the grandchild of immigrants. There are mistranslations, but also missed translations of never really knowing the whole story. And of course, there is also the worrying of love, and humour.

I also created a copy of the film with no music, which feels more intimate to me.
